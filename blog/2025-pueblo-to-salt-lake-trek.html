2025 Pueblo to Salt Lake Trek

The three detached groups of the Mormon Battalion were sent to Pueblo Colorado to winter with the group of Mississippi Saints. They left Pueblo in April 1847 and made their way to the Salt Lake Valley arriving shortly after Brigham Young's Vanguard Company.

This year from April to July, the Hensons plan to retrace this group's path to learn more about the trail and highlight the significance of the Mormon and Battalion Trail history.
